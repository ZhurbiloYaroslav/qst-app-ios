Let's Quit Smoking Together has existed as a Public Non-Profit NGO for six years already. It is funded by Aleksei Koval—the author of the eponymous book—and from donations of those concerned about the fate of nicotine-dependent people.

We make every effort to popularise healthy lifestyle and help people in Ukraine and Europe fight tobacco, alcohol, and drug addictions.

Over these years, thousands of promotions were held, as well as dozens of roundtables, and several tours around Ukraine to popularise healthy lifestyle.

For more event reports, please visit our website www.quitsmokingtogether.org

Now we announce a fundraising campaign to get the Quit Smoking Together book translated into ten languages:

- French

- German

- Chinese

- Japanese

- Arabic

- Persian

- Italian

- Spanish

- Romanian

- Belarusian

And this is just the beginning.

We plan on:

1) Creating e-books available for free to anyone in the world wishing to get rid of nicotine addiction.

2) Integrating these languages into our Android and iOS apps to make the book easier to read, the materials to work with, promotions to launch, and conferences on adolescent smoking prevention to hold.

3) Translating Aleksei Koval’s book Mom, I Don’t Smoke that prepares a teenager to being offered a smoke and parents to properly supporting their kids.

Our plans are ambitious, but, alas, there’s little we can do by ourselves.

We would like to ask you for any help you can provide to save lives—a person who quits smoking may live for up to ten or twenty years longer, after all, and it is you who can give these extra years of life to them. These people will be able to see their children, grandchildren, do something good, plant a tree, build a house, and help those in need.

Saving a life is easy. Donate and you may help save someone’s life.

It’s so simple but so much. It’s all in your hands!

Your help is important to us! Dozens of people die from nicotine addiction every day. Helping us, you may save someone’s life!
